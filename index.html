About Us

We began as a Cargo services in 2012 AIM logistics and cardo services, In 2021 we are sister consult company of AIM Logistics. Aim Logistics is a growing and forward thinking company, in the field of International Freight Forwarding, Shipping and Consolidation. We have been successfully handling consignments of different types of cargoes ranging from Engineering Goods, Chemicals, Pharma and Food Grains using our three-pronged mode of transportation, viz. Sea Transport, Air Transport and Combination of Air-Sea Transport. We offer our valuable customers fast, reliable and cost effective logistics services, customized to their specific requirements. We offer complete range of services like Transportation, Clearance and Freight Forwarding solutions to our customers.

Road Transportation

Aim Logistics offers efficient ground transportation by way of road. This forms an integral aspect of our concept of door-to-door delivery. Over the years we have built up strong relationships with trucking companies in the remotest parts of the country. We leverage such relationships to ensure that we offer our customers with the safest and the fastest transportation of their cargo at the best possible rates.
Our worldwide agents have similar contacts with reliable local ground transportation companies which enable us to provide our customers with professional ground transportation services all over the world.

Our integrated ground logistics solutions include:
  • Inland transportation by Road
  • Loading of the cargo in the safest possible manner at the point of pick-up
  • Unloading of the cargo at the point of destination for further transportation or final delivery as the case may be
Custom Clearance

Aim Logistics has been providing professional Customs Clearance Services, thereby making it one of the most experienced players in the Indian market. At the back of our cordial relations, we are confident of assuring you speedy export and import clearances and quick release of consignments in case of imports or the relevant Bills of Lading in case of exports.

Our integrated ground logistics solutions include:
  • Documentation for Customs Clearance
  • Carting of the goods
  • Examination of the Shipment
  • Customs Clearance of the import cargo at the port of entry
  • Handling of the cargo at the port
  • Arranging for a Bonded Warehouse as and when required by the customer
  • Cargo stuffing & de-stuffing at Ports, ICDs and Customers’ warehouse
